About Me

Great experience in Unity development, VR development, 3D graphic design, and animation, highly skilled and versatile professional in the field of digital media. With a passion for cutting-edge technology and a talent for bringing ideas to life, has built a strong reputation as an innovator and problem-solver.

As a Unity developer, worked on a variety of projects, ranging from mobile games and interactive experiences to virtual and augmented reality applications. With expertise in C# programming, UI design, and game mechanics, ability to create immersive and engaging experiences that keep users coming back for more.

In addition to my development work, I am also a skilled 3D graphic designer and animator. I have created high-quality 3D models and animations for a wide range of applications, from product demos and marketing materials to explainer videos and interactive presentations.

What I Do

XR (VR/AR/MR) Engineering

Developing high-fidelity, cross-platform immersive applications for Meta Quest, Pico, and PC VR headsets using Unity and OpenXR.

Immersive Training Simulations

Designing interactive, physics-based VR learning modules and safety training solutions replacing traditional corporate coursework.

Performance Optimization

Executing GPU/CPU profiling, draw call batching, LOD implementation, and shader optimization to securely hit 72+ FPS targets on standalone hardware.

Interactive 3D Environments

Building real-time, interactive 3D digital spaces using Unity with advanced UI Toolkit implementations and dynamic narrative systems.

3D Asset Pipeline

Modeling, UV unwrapping, rigging, and skeletal animation of optimal 3D assets using Blender, fully integrated for game engines.

Mobile & Cross-Platform

Technical Leadership

Leading XR development teams, managing agile sprint lifecycles, structured code reviews, and scheduling delivery milestones.

Spatial UI/UX Design

Architecting intuitive, user-centric spatial interfaces specifically designed for VR workflows using the modern Unity XR Interaction Toolkit.

Multiplayer Architecture

Integrating robust networking solutions for shared interactive experiences using FishNet, Photon (PUN), Fusion 2, and Unity Netcode.
